HackAD is a student interest group dedicated to supporting tech enthusiasts at New York University Abu Dhabi. Throughout the year, we organize technical workshops, discussion panels on internships, trips to tech hubs in the region, and the annual HackAD hackathon. Everyone, from all year levels and coding backgrounds, is welcome!
